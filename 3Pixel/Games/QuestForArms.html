Sharpman Games - Quest For Arms

Type Of Game: Precision Platformer, Short but Sweet
Age Rating: 13+ (includes some explicit scenes with high blood intensity)
Story Description: You're name is Scrub. It was just a normal day at your school, until your human biology class. It turns out that your teacher - Doctor Ambrósio - is planning to amputate your arms for his own gain. After doing so, he dumps Scrub in the wilderness full with Doctor Ambrósio's creations.

Gameplay Goals: Quest for Arms is short but sweet precision platformer where you got your arms amputated by a crazy scientist. You now have to use your legs and powerups to fight the scientist's creations until you reach him.
Play Hours: About 45 minutes to 1 hour.
Updates: Yes a couple new levels and bug fixes.

About Game From Devs:
n3cr0jr: Quest for Arms doesn't provide much content, with only 3 levels and a boss fight, but our plan wasn't to make the most complete game, our plan was to put as much polish into those 3 levels as possible. The definition of Short but Sweet.

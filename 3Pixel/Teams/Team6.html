Sharpman Games

Status: Active

Team Info: Sharpman Games is a videogame developer that started in September 2019 with only 2 members: Fernando Sardinha and Ruben Franco. Within a month of research and learning they made one game called Kill the Monsters! using Construct 2. From November 2019 to March 2021 they gained 2 new members: Tigre (in February 2020) and Francisco Alves (in March 2021). In April 2021 Sharpman Games was put under 3Pixel Studios where they have been developing Quest for Arms using Godot Game Engine. In the future, Sharpman Games intends to make games to appeal to the more hardcore audience, while still keeping the games casual enough so casual audiences still enjoy our games.
